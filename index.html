🚀 Publish This Site for Free

Step-by-step guide to host this tracker online — accessible from any device, forever free.

1
Save this file to your computer

Right-click this page in your browser → Save As → save as index.html. Or download from Claude's output. Keep all your data — it's saved in your browser's localStorage.

2
Create a free GitHub account

Go to github.com → click Sign up → complete registration. It's completely free.

3
Create a new repository

After logging in, click the ＋ icon at the top right → New repository. Name it paytrack. Set it to Public. Click Create repository.

4
Upload your index.html

In your new repo, click Add file → Upload files. Drag your index.html file into the box. Scroll down and click Commit changes.

5
Enable GitHub Pages

Go to your repo → click Settings (top nav) → scroll to Pages in the left sidebar. Under Source, select Deploy from a branch. Choose main branch and / (root). Click Save.

6
Your site is live! 🎉

After 1–2 minutes, your tracker will be available at: https://[your-username].github.io/paytrack. Bookmark it on your phone and desktop. Data is stored in your browser — no login needed.

⚠️
Important: notifications work per device

Because this is a browser-based app, you'll need to click "Enable Notifications" once on each device you use. Notifications are scheduled locally in your browser — they don't rely on any external server.
